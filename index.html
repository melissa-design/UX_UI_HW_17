Melissa Vaezi

Hi, I'm Melissa Vaezi, a Graduate UX/UI Designer. I am passionate about user research and testing because I believe these are fundamental to good user experience. I believe in seeking to understand the experience of all potential users and including them in the design process. Once there is solid UX process undertaken, beautiful and functional UI solutions can be built that meet the needs of all users. I'm excited to apply and build on the comprehensive skills I've gained in the UI/UX course I've just completed at the University of Adelaide through real-world projects.

Technical Skills: Design Thinking Skills, Figma, Adobe XD, HTML, CSS, Java Script
